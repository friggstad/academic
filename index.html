Research Interests

My research interests primarily concern combinatorial optimization with emphasis on the design of approximation algorithms for NP-hard combinatorial optimization problems. This includes, but is not limited to, problems in data clustering, vehicle routing, network design.

Programming Club!

I also facilitate our "Programming Club", which is a group of students interested in algorithmic problem algorithmic problem solving. The main activity of this club is solving problems like one encounters at the ICPC, Google Code Jam / Kick Start, and Codeforces.

If you find this interesting, even if at a casual (non-competitive) level, do consider joining our mailing list. A link to the list can be found at the club information page.
